Hi there... I hope you've been enjoying Edly. I appreciate all your support for Edly, and I'm sure that I will keep maintaining this project as long as MCPS is still using this dumb tool. Thanks again for your support! Directly message me or shoot an email to developer@mingjie.info if you have any suggestions, corrections, or ideas!
